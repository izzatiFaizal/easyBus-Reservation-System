About

Looking for a top-notch bus ticket online booking system? We easyBus can provide to you what you are looking for. This easyBus website have been designed to ease the process of checking bus schedule and buying ticket at the counter. This system is developed to provide information that could be important to a particular traveler when they’re looking to book a bus ticket online.

There are some popular bus terminal in Malaysia that act as pick-up and drop-off point for several routes. Travelers can choose from any of these terminal to be their pick-up or drop-off point and ride the bus at their convenience.

Popular Bus Terminal:

  • Terminal Bersepadu Selatan(TBS)
  • Terminal 1
  • Melaka Sentral
  • JB Larkin Bus Terminal
  • Pudu Sentral Puduraya
  • Berjaya Times Square
  • Sungai Nibong
  • Ipoh Amanjaya
  • Kuantan Sentral

Below are the lists of bus companies that are available on easyBus. With help of these companies travelers can also choose the bus they are comfortable with to ride on.

Bus operators that are involved:

  • Transtar Express

Steps to make your booking

Below are the steps to follow when booking your ticket:

  • First, you need to login to your easyBus account.
  • Second, check the bus schedule.
  • Third, book your ticket.
  • Forth, show your booking details that has the booking ID at the ticket counter.
  • Fifth, make your payment and get your ticket.
